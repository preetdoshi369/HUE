Nurturing Client Relationships and Fostering Innovation:
A Behind-the-Scenes Look at Our Growth in the Digital Marketing Arena

In 2017, we embarked on an exciting journey to forge our own path in the digital marketing agency landscape. Before you dive into our story, we’d like to share a little secret: our founders and co-founders come from diverse and successful backgrounds in banking, hospitality, and aviation. These experiences taught us invaluable lessons from some of the best mentors in the industry, emphasizing the importance of putting our people first.

However, we soon realized that starting a branding & marketing agency required more than just expertise and passion. Our journey began in a modest home office, with a core team of just three members who recognized a significant gap in the market. While every business aspired to become a recognizable brand, marketing often ranked low on their spending priorities. We decided to change that. We partnered closely with our clients—who we proudly call friends today—creating a collaborative spirit where we were equally invested in their success.

What’s next? We needed more people—creative thinkers with bold ideas and the ambition to share them with the world. Our team grew and evolved, mirroring the ever-changing digital landscape. From a humble trio, we blossomed into a team of 30, and today we are proud to say that we have come a long way.

As we diversified our services, we collaborated with some of the brightest creative minds in the industry, forming a dedicated team that embodies innovation and excellence. With growth came success, and we are thrilled to share our achievements with our internal family, fostering a culture of teamwork and celebration.

Today, Happy Us Entertainment LLP operates as a comprehensive digital marketing agency, equipped to meet all your needs under one roof. Whether it’s creative design, compelling storytelling, stunning visual production, audience targeting, website development, or search engine optimization, we have you covered!

As we share our story with you, remember that this is not the conclusion but rather a new chapter—one where you can become an integral part of our journey. We invite you to join us as we continue to innovate, inspire, and create remarkable brands together through our collaborative marketing solutions that drive business growth and success.
