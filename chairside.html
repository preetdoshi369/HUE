Overview

Chairside is a leading provider of dental equipment, specializing in high-quality dental chairs and advanced digital solutions such as the I Scan Pro and 3Shape TRIOS scanners. Recently, Chairside has merged with Invisalign, further solidifying its presence in the dental industry. With a commitment to enhancing dental practices through state-of-the-art technology, Chairside also offers educational content via their YouTube channel, @chairsideeducation, which boasts over 26,000 subscribers.

Challenges

  • Chairside sought to strengthen its online presence, effectively monetize its educational content, and boost brand awareness within the dental community. They engaged Happy Us Entertainment to develop a comprehensive digital strategy that would elevate their brand and drive engagement across multiple platforms.

    • What Happy Us Did for Chairside: Happy Us Entertainment implemented a multifaceted approach to enhance Chairside's digital footprint:
    Website Development:

  • Designed and developed a user-friendly website that showcases Chairside's product offerings, integrates educational content, and provides seamless navigation for potential clients.

    • Social Media Content Creation:

  • Produced engaging and informative content tailored for platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn to highlight Chairside's products and industry expertise.

    • YouTube Monetization:

  • Assisted in optimizing Chairside's YouTube channel, @chairsideeducation, by focusing on monetization strategies and increasing subscriber engagement.

    • Brand Positioning:

  • Brand Positioning: Developed a cohesive brand identity that differentiates Chairside in the competitive dental equipment market, emphasizing their dual focus on quality products and educational resources.

The Impact:

    Post-collaboration, Chairside experienced significant growth in online engagement and brand recognition:
  • Enhanced Online Presence:

    The new website and revitalized social media profiles attracted a broader audience, leading to increased inquiries and sales.

    • YouTube Growth:

  • After Happy Us took over, the @chairsideeducation channel experienced a substantial boost, with a rise in subscriber count, improved viewer interaction, and increased ad revenue, significantly expanding its reach within the dental community.

    • Brand Authority:

  • Chairside's consistent and professional online presence established them as a trusted authority in dental equipment and education, fostering stronger relationships with dental professionals.

Through strategic digital initiatives and leveraging the merger with Invisalign, Happy Us Entertainment successfully transformed Chairside's online presence, enabling them to effectively monetize their content and solidify their position in the dental industry.
