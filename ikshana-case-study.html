Overview

Nestled in the tranquil valleys of Khandala, the Ikshana Resort and Spa by Kohinoor Hospitality stands as a testament to reimagined luxury. In a region where resorts and hotels compete to blend with the picturesque surroundings of Khandala and Lonavala, the challenge is to stand out as a premium destination. At Happy Us Entertainment, we embarked on a journey to position Ikshana Resort as a beacon of understated elegance, ensuring it resonated with the ethos of calm luxury. By combining our expertise in branding, design, and storytelling, we created a holistic experience that elevated Ikshana beyond its competition, making it a destination rather than just another resort.…

Challanges

  • Rebranding a Historic Institution

    Ikshana Resort began its journey as Kohinoor School of Hotel Management, a well-regarded institution. Transitioning from a school to a premium hospitality brand required a complete reinvention, preserving its legacy while aligning with its new identity.

  • Capturing the Calm of Khandala

    In a region characterized by serene valleys and cool breezes, the challenge was to create a brand that mirrored the natural tranquility and stood apart in a market saturated with conventional offerings.

  • Seamless Integration of Architecture and Branding

    As the property took shape, aligning architectural and interior design elements with the brand vision required a deep collaboration with architects, designers, and the management team.

Solution

  • Crafting a Brand Identity

    Designed a logo that embodied Ikshana’s ethos of calm luxury, drawing inspiration from Khandala's breezy valleys. The free-flowing, elegant fonts mirrored the resort's serene yet sophisticated ambiance. Developed comprehensive brand guidelines, including color palettes, font choices, brand story, and tonality, to maintain consistency across all touchpoints.

  • Collaborating on Interior Branding

    Partnered with architects and interior designers to ideate internal signage, uniform designs, and in-room amenities, ensuring a cohesive brand experience. Paid attention to details, aligning design elements like signage placements and aesthetic choices with the overall theme of tranquility.

Immersive Visuals

Immersive Visuals and Storytelling

Conducted a high-quality photoshoot featuring wide-angle and architectural shots, complemented by drone photography that captured the serene beauty of the valley and the resort's elegance. Produced videos showcasing the resort’s allure, crafting a compelling visual narrative.

Website

Building a User-Centric Websites

Designed an intuitive and visually appealing website that reflected the resort's premium nature, offering seamless navigation to enhance user experience.

Social Media

Highlighted key features and amenities to position the resort as a luxurious getaway in Khandala.
