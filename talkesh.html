Overview

Takesh Singh is a highly accomplished Public Speaking and Camera-Facing Coach with nearly 23 years of experience in the media and entertainment industry. Known for his ability to empower individuals to find their authentic voice, Takesh specializes in coaching corporate leaders, creators, and personal brands in mastering the art of impactful communication on stage and in front of the camera. Beyond coaching, his expertise spans acting, filmmaking, and branded content creation, with notable contributions to acclaimed films, series, and projects like The Tashkent Files, Gehraiyaan, and the Cannes Lion-winning Kaan Khajura Tesan campaign.

Challenges

  • Takesh sought out Happy Us Entertainment to amplify his personal brand as a public speaking and media trainer. While his expertise and experience were unparalleled, he needed a strategic partner to position his offerings effectively in the digital space, differentiate between his identity as an actor and a public speaking coach, and create a cohesive narrative for his diverse portfolio. This led to the conceptualization of "Let's Talkesh,” an Instagram profile and dedicated platform for showcasing his coaching services and professional journey.

    • What Happy Us Did for Takesh Singh:

    Happy Us Entertainment crafted a comprehensive strategy for Takesh, focusing on brand building and content differentiation. This included:

  • Developing a professional and engaging online presence, particularly on LinkedIn, to highlight his dual roles as a coach and actor.

  • Creating and designing the website, which serves as a dedicated platform for his public speaking and camera-facing coaching services. The website includes features for one-on-one sessions and workshop bookings, enabling him to monetise his expertise effectively.

  • Launching and managing the "Let's Talkesh” Instagram profile to showcase his work, including coaching highlights, testimonials, and thought leadership content.

  • Designing a content calendar that showcased his expertise through thought leadership posts, client success stories, and behind-the-scenes glimpses of his workshops.

  • Producing video content to highlight his coaching techniques, testimonials from clients, and his journey in the media industry.

  • Differentiating his personal brand as an actor from his professional persona as a coach, ensuring clarity for his audience.

The Impact:

Before partnering with Happy Us, Takesh’s reach was limited primarily to word-of-mouth referrals and his immediate professional network. Post-collaboration, his visibility and engagement on LinkedIn and Instagram increased significantly. The "Let's Talks" website became a pivotal tool for monetizing his sessions, attracting a diverse clientele, including international corporate leaders and creators. His workshops saw increased demand, and his content resonated with his target audience. Notably, he was recognized as a LinkedIn Top Voice, decoding the platform's strategies to further establish his authority. The strategic storytelling and branding helped him transition from a seasoned coach to a recognized thought leader in public speaking, while maintaining his distinct identity as an actor.
