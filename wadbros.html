Overview

Wadbros Exhaust Fans has been a key player in the exhaust fan manufacturing industry for over 25 years. With a robust offline network of dealers in major metropolitan areas and tier 2 cities, the company has established a solid foundation. However, they face challenges in brand recognition compared to competitors like GM Electrical, Crompton, and Atomberg.

Challanges

  • Lack of Brand Recognition:

    Despite their long-standing presence, Wadbros's brand recognition is significantly lower than that of its competitors.

  • Multiple Domains:

    The company's traditional operations have resulted in multiple domain names, negatively impacting their visibility and search engine optimization (SEO) efforts.

  • Inconsistent Branding:

    Each location presents the Wadbros brand differently, using various colors and fonts, leading to a disjointed brand identity.

  • B2B Outreach Difficulties:

    As a B2B entity, Wadbros struggles to effectively reach potential dealerships, architects, and key personnel.

  • Exhibition Presence:

    Although they participate in recognized exhibitions across India, their booth presence has not created a strong impact.

    • Solutions

  • Modern Website Development:

    Redesigned a user-friendly website that caters to both rural dealers and modern architects, enhancing brand engagement.

  • Domain Consolidation:

    Streamlined the company’s multiple domains to improve SEO performance and boost online visibility.

  • Standardized Branding Materials:

    Developed a cohesive packaging design and brochure, ensuring consistent branding across all locations.

  • Strategic Lead Generation Campaign:

    Planned and executed a targeted lead generation campaign, collaborating with relevant YouTube influencers during exhibitions.

    • Results

  • Optimized Website:

    Launched a new, fully-optimized website showcasing over 100 products in a modern format to effectively compete with industry rivals.

  • Improved Domain Authority:

    Redirected multiple domains to the parent website, improving visibility and domain authority significantly.

  • Enhanced SEO Performance:

    Conducted an extensive SEO audit and implemented strategies that positioned www.wadbros.com at the top of search results for brand-specific keywords.

  • Effective Google Ads Campaign:

    Executed detailed research for Google Ads, generating three conversions for every Rs. 1,000 spent on advertising.

  • Consistent Brand Brochure:

    Designed a standardized brochure now utilized by all dealers and local offices across India, alongside uniform seasonal greetings that reflect the brand identity.

  • Revamped Exhibition Booths:

    Redesigned exhibition stalls according to modern trends, adhering to brand guidelines for colors and fonts.

  • Impactful Exhibition Results:

    The latest exhibition in December 2024 saw significant success, with the engagement of three top-performing YouTubers, leading to a spike in social media followers from 200 to 1,300 within three days.

  • Increased Inquiries:

    Content uploaded on YouTube now generates a minimum of 30 potential inquiries daily via phone calls.
